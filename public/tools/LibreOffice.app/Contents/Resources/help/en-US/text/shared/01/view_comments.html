View Comments

Show or hide a document's comments and replies to them.

To access this command...

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Comments.

On the left of the horizontal ruler

Click Comments.

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Comments.

From the context menu:

Choose Show Comment.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Review - Show All Comments.

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Comments.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Review - Show Comments.

From the menu bar:

Choose View - Comments.

From the tabbed interface:

Choose Review - Show Comments.

From toolbars:

Show Comments
